Halloween Costumes

Both children and adults wear a wide range of costumes representing monsters to pop culture characters to objects on Halloween. Costumes were traditionally worn on Halloween stemming from the belief that this is the night that supernatural beings such as ghosts and ghouls, demons and monsters roam the earth so people must also blend in with them.
